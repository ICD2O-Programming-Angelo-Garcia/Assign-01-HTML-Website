What Are Succulents?

Succulents are plants that store water in their leaves, stems, or roots, allowing them to survive in dry environments. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, making them popular for both indoor and outdoor gardening.

Care Tips

  • Water sparingly – only when the soil is dry.
  • Place them in bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Use well-draining pots and soil.
  • Be patient – they grow slowly, but they thrive in the right conditions!
